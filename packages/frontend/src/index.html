Welcome to XivGear!

xivgear.app is an advanced FFXIV gear planner and calculator. Build, compare, calculate, and simulate your sets without complicated spreadsheets.

Here's a few of the features it supports:

  • For most jobs, DPS simulations built right into the gear set editor, for instant feedback.
  • Share a sheet containing multiple sets, so players can view them at a glance, rather than having to click through several sets.
  • The ability to solve the optimal melds and food for a given set of gear.

Happy Raiding! Don't forget to check out The Balance for best-in-slot gear sets.
